## What It Does

### 📄 Upload Your Slides → Get Instant Questions
Drop in a PDF or PowerPoint from any lecture. PastCS's AI scans the content, detects the topic, and generates exam-style questions on the spot. No typing. No guessing what to study.

### 🧠 Practice the Way Exams Work
- **30 random questions** drawn from across all topics in a course
- Single choice, multiple choice, and fill-in-the-blank — just like your real paper
- Timed **Exam Simulation** mode that mimics the pressure of the actual exam

### ✅ Know Exactly What You Got Wrong (and Why)
Every answer you submit comes with:
- Instant feedback — correct or incorrect, right away
- **AI-powered explanations** — the AI tutor breaks down *why* the answer is correct, why the wrong options are wrong, gives you a real-world example, and flags the common mistakes students make
- Your session is saved automatically — pause, come back, and pick up where you left off

### 🤖 Ask the AI Tutor Anything
Not just for practice. The built-in AI Tutor can:
- Teach you any topic from scratch, step-by-step
- Give you examples and analogies
- Point you to articles and YouTube videos so you actually understand — not just memorise

### 📊 Track Your Progress
See your scores, streaks, and improvement over time. Know which topics need more attention before it's too late.
